Advanced Sales Dashboard for a Retail Store.

We collaborated with a forward-thinking retail company to craft six comprehensive dashboards leveraging their rich historical data. These dashboards cover critical aspects of their operations, facilitating informed decision-making and strategic planning.

  1. Customer Retargeting Dashboard: Unlocking insights into customer behavior and preferences, this dashboard provides valuable information for targeted marketing and retention strategies.

  2. Order Analysis Dashboard: By dissecting order data, this dashboard offers deep insights into purchase patterns, order frequency, and average order value, empowering the company to optimize their sales processes.

  3. Product Insight Dashboard: Analyzing product performance metrics such as sales volume, revenue, and profitability, this dashboard aids in inventory management, product assortment decisions, and pricing strategies.

  4. Sales Teams Dashboard: Visualizing sales team performance metrics and KPIs, this dashboard facilitates performance evaluation, resource allocation, and incentive planning.

  5. Store Location Dashboard: By mapping sales performance across different store locations, this dashboard provides insights into geographical trends, customer demographics, and regional opportunities.

  6. Time Range Analysis Dashboard: Analyzing sales data over different time ranges (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly), this dashboard uncovers seasonal trends, sales peaks, and troughs, aiding in forecasting and resource planning.

These dashboards represent a transformative step towards data-driven decision-making, enabling the retail company to stay agile, competitive, and customer-centric in today's dynamic market landscape. It was a privilege to collaborate with them on this journey toward operational excellence and growth.

Data Dashboard Creation for Banex Cycles

I had the privilege of collaborating with Banex Cycles to develop two impactful dashboards that harness the potential of their historical data. Using the versatile tool, PowerBI, I created two essential insights: "Continent Sales vs. Target" and "Product Profit." 1. Continent Sales vs. Target Insight: In this dynamic dashboard, we showcased key performance indicators (KPIs) vital to Banex Cycles' success. The KPIs included Quantity Sold, Total Revenue, Profit Margin, and Percentage Profit Margin. Visually, we presented Sales Revenue by continent versus Target, revealing geographical insights, and identified the Top N products and sub-categories by Revenue, providing a clear path for growth. 2. Product Profit Insight: Our second dashboard delved into the intricacies of product profitability. We dissected profit data by weekday, month, and year, offering valuable insights into product performance trends over time. These interactive and visually engaging dashboards are a testament to the power of data-driven decision-making, giving Banex Cycles the tools they need to thrive in a competitive market. It was a privilege to work with them to unlock these insights and empower their business for a brighter future.
